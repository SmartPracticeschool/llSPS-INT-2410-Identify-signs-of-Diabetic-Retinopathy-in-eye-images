Diabetic Retinopathy Detection and Identification of Level's


Retinopathy is an eye disease caused by diabetes that can lead to loss of vision or even complete blindness.Diabetic retinopathy accounts for 12% of all new cases of blindness in the United States, and is the leading cause of blindness for people aged 20 to 64 years. Diabetic retinopathy is a diabetes complication that affects eyes. It's caused by damage to the blood vessels of the light-sensitive tissue at the back of the eye (retina) Millions of people suffer from diabetic retinopathy, the leading cause of blindness among working aged adults.
